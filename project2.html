Looking to track your meals? Need some suggestions on what to eat?
PantryMate is a small little program designed to offer you many features in regards to your meal plan.
Here you can get meal and grocery suggestions based off of nutritional goals, and see your weekly and monthly progress from our charts and graphs.
The PantryMate will also notify you when your food selections are low as well as offering up different recipies for your use.


PantryMate is a work in progress that was created in the Summer of 2017 by four students at Point Park University.
If you have any questions or concerns in regards to PantryMate, do not hesitate to contact us!

INGREDIENTSINSTRUCTIONS
- 4 bell peppers, seeds and cores removed
- 1 tablespoon of extra-virgin olive oil
- Kosher salt
- Freshly ground black pepper
- 3 tablespoons of butter
- 1/2 large onion, chopped
- 2 cloves garlic
- 3 cups of shredded rotisserie chicken
- 1/2 cup of hot sauce
- 2 cups of shredded gouda
- Ranch dressing, for drizzling
- 2 tablespoons of freshly chopped chives		 - 1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees. Place bell peppers cut side-up on a large baking sheet and drizzle all over with olive oil, then season with salt and pepper.
- 2. In a large skiller over medium heat, melt butter. Add onion and cook until tender, about 5 minutes. Add garlic and cook until fragrent, 1 minute more.
- 3. Add shredded chicken and hot sauce until combined. Cook until the mixture comes to a simmer, then remove fromm heat.
- 4. Divide chicken mixture between pepper halves. Top each with gouda and bake until cheese is melted and peppers are crisp-tender, about 20 minutes.
- 5. Drizzle each stuffed pepper with ranch and sprinkle with chives.
